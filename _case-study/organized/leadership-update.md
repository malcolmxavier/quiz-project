## Basecamp Rewards — Customer Feedback Review (Jan–Apr)
**Date:** 2026-04-19
**From:** [New Program Manager]

### Status
At Risk

### Summary
Four months of customer feedback show a clear pattern: customers love Basecamp, but feel nothing about Basecamp Rewards. Satisfaction is trending down and the program lacks brand identity, personalization, and any real reason to engage. Early hypothesis: the fix is a personality-driven relaunch, not another points tweak.

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Current (Apr) | Target | Trend |
|--------|---------------|--------|-------|
| Rewards Satisfaction (Very Sat + Sat) | 34% | 60%+ | Down (from 46% in Jan) |
| Rewards Neutral Share | 48% | <25% | Up |
| Basecamp Overall NPS | 67 | 60+ | Flat (strong) |
| Basecamp Rewards NPS | 12 | 40+ | Weak |
| App Avg Session Time (Mar) | 23 sec | 60+ sec | Flat |
| Rewards Redeemed (Mar) | 12 | — | Very low |

### Progress Since Last Update
- Synthesized 4 months of survey, in-store, and social feedback into a single diagnosis document.
- Identified the recurring customer phrase — *"fine but forgettable"* — appearing in 3 separate months.
- Mapped the gap between Basecamp brand strength (NPS 67) and Rewards program weakness (NPS 12).
- Surfaced a promising direction from handoff notes + customer quotes: a personality/identity-driven experience (e.g., "coffee personality") rather than more points.

### Blockers / Risks
- **Declining satisfaction trend** — Very/Satisfied dropped from 46% → 34% over 4 months. Left unaddressed, this accelerates.
- **No brand personality in the program** — customers explicitly say it could be "any chain." We are losing the brand moat.
- **Tier system isn't landing** — neither customers nor baristas can explain the tiers. Any fix will likely need to rethink this.
- **Competitive pressure** — Starbucks (gamification) and Dutch Bros (relationships) are actively pulling rewards-minded customers.

### Next Steps
- Finalize the problem diagnosis and share with Dana this week.
- Dig into the competitor research and member-data CSV to pressure-test the hypothesis.
- Draft 2–3 program concepts centered on identity/personalization for leadership review.
- Target: concept recommendation within 2 weeks.

### Decision Needed
None yet — diagnostic phase. A concept recommendation and budget ask will follow in the next update.
